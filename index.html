Welcome

The Mat Factory is one of the premier Wrestling Clubs in Western Pennsylvania today. Between the training and development of young aspiring wrestlers from youth to Collegiate athletes and the rise of a steady stream of Professional MMA fighters, the Mat Factory offers elite Instruction and coaching in Folkstyle Wrestling, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and MMA. Our facility provides over 90 square feet of mat space with other cross-fit type equipment available to supplement our training regimen.
Chris Como, former PIAA Coach of the Year and NCAA All-American and Isaac Greeley, 2x NCAA All-American and Hall of Fame Inductee, both anchor a coaching staff that now includes Burrell Head Wrestling Coach and Kittanning Head Wrestling Coach, Josh Shields and Brandon Newill, both NCAA Champions. Former NCAA All-American and PIAA Champion Jordan Shields has also joined as our Head Instructor for the Youth program. Coupled with Bellator Fighter, Dom Mazzotta and Jiu-Jitsu Instructor Todd Mussman, the growing resume of PIAA and NCAA Placewinners and Champions let the results speak for themselves.
